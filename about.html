From the Job Site to Your Doorstep

221|

PrimeWash Gear Australia was born out of pure frustration. Edinson — known to mates as Antonio — is a Melbourne-based tradie who runs a paving and property maintenance business. Day in, day out, he relies on his pressure washer to get the job done.

222|

But every time a nozzle wore out, a fitting cracked, or a hose sprung a leak, he'd find himself either overpaying at the big hardware chains or waiting weeks for cheap online imports that fell apart after one use.

223|

So he did what any self-respecting tradie would do — he started his own supply. PrimeWash Gear was created to give real tradies access to quality pressure washer parts at prices that make sense. No inflated markups. No questionable quality. Just gear that works, sourced and tested by someone who actually uses it.

The Problem We're Fixing

242|

Australian tradies have been getting ripped off for years. Here's how:

243|

🏪 The Big Box Stores — Bunnings and Total Tools carry a handful of parts, but the selection is limited and the prices are inflated. They're not specialists — pressure washer parts are an afterthought.

244|

📦 eBay & Amazon Imports — Sure, you can find a nozzle for $6 on eBay. But it's made from pot metal that strips on day two. You end up spending more in the long run.

245|

🏭 Commercial Suppliers — They've got the good gear, but they only talk to businesses ordering in bulk. Good luck getting a single fitting from them as a solo operator.

246|

PrimeWash Gear fills the gap. Real parts, real stock in Melbourne, and prices that don't make you wince. We ship to tradies all over Australia — from Broome to Hobart.
